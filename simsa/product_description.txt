Korean Word Lookup - Korean Translator for Learners

Read Korean web pages without breaking your flow.

Korean Word Lookup is a lightweight Chrome extension for Korean learners. Hover over Korean words to see quick English meanings, or select a full sentence to translate it in one shot.

Best for:
- Korean learners reading articles, posts, docs, and study pages
- People who want quick English meaning without opening another tab
- Daily reading practice on Chrome, Windows, Mac, and Chromebook

Main features

Hover word lookup
- Hover over Korean words to see English translations
- Choose Google Translate for speed or KRDICT Korean Dictionary for detailed definitions
- Auto-Fallback can try the other source when one source has no result
- Clean tooltip with optional Korean definitions

Selection translation
- Select Korean sentences or paragraphs to translate them instantly
- Turn selection translation on in the popup or with a shortcut
- Useful when word-by-word meaning is not enough

Controls and shortcuts
- One-click ON/OFF toggle
- Windows/Linux/ChromeOS: Alt+T, Alt+G, Alt+K, Alt+S
- Mac: Option+Shift+T, Option+Shift+G, Option+Shift+K, Option+Shift+S
- Customize hover delay, compact tooltip, and definition count

Privacy
- Only the text you hover or select is sent to Google Translate or KRDICT to fetch translations or definitions
- Settings are saved in Chrome storage
- No raw hovered or selected text is sent to product analytics
